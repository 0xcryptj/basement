🔥 Deflationary Mechanics

A portion of every game fee is permanently burned, reducing total supply over time. This creates upward price pressure.

Entry fees for major tournaments include a burn component. Bigger events = bigger burns.

Platform profits are used to buy back tokens from the market and burn them permanently.
